Neural Network channles

Input Dimensions: 25 channels
- 10 frames with time distance=0.1s of the previous 1s position of agents
- 10 frames with time distance=0.1s of the previous 1s position of Autonomous Vehicle
- 2 current frames of positions of both agents and AV
- 3 channels of semantic map R,G,B

Output Dimensions: 100 channels
- 50 frames of next 5s position of agents
- Represent in two coordinates (x,y axis)

Resnet34

EfficientNet B3

Stacking of Resnet & EfficientNet

Ensemble Network with stacking where model A is ResNet 34 and model B is EfficientNet b3.
The Generalizer contains 1 nn.flatten, 1 ReLU and 2 nn.linear layers that combine the two model and then reduce to the correct output dimensions
