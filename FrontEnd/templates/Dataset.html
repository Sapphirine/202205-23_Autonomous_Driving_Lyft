DataSET

We adopted Level 5 dataset released by Lyft:Â 
- Currently the largest collection of travel agent motion
- A total of 170,000 scenes capturing the environment around the autonomous vehicle
- 1000+ hours of traffic agent movement
- 16,000 miles of data from 23 vehicle
- 150,000 semantic map annotations

For our project, the key datatype from the Lyft level 5 includes Scenes, Frames, Agents, and Traffic Light Phase.
- Scenes includes: References to its corresponding frames in terms of the start and end index within the frames array.
- Frames includes: All information that was observed at a given time stamp with regard to agents and traffic light.
- Agents includes: Ego (The AV) and traffic agents (Cyclists, Pedestrians, Other Vehicles), and Each entry describes the object in terms of its attributes such as position and velocity, gives the agent a tracking number to track it over multiple frames and its most probable label.
